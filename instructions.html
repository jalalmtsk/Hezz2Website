How to Play Hezz2 Star

Follow these instructions to master the game!

  1. Start the Game: Click on the "Play Now" button on the home page.
  2. Choose Your Mode: Select from Adventure, Challenge, or Multiplayer.
  3. Learn Controls: Use arrow keys or swipe gestures to move your character.
  4. Complete Quests: Finish missions to earn points and rewards.
  5. Unlock Rewards: Collect coins, power-ups, and special items to progress.
  6. Check Leaderboards: See how you rank against other players.
  7. Stay Updated: Check the updates page for new events and challenges.

Pro Tips

  • Focus on completing daily missions for bonus rewards.
  • Join a team in multiplayer mode for cooperative challenges.
  • Use power-ups strategically to maximize your score.
  • Practice regularly to improve your reflexes and timing.
